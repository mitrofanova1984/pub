About/Contact

Svetlana Mitrofanova studied illustration in Sint Luca's school of arts under the supervision of Hoele Dewanckel, campus Gent. Participated in a number of exhibitions in Gent. The artist uses different media in her work, such as visual art, text and perfomance. The main focus of Svetlana's research  is the human duality. With originally Russian background, she varies and adapts aesthetic codes, which are well understood both in Western and Eastern Europe. Svetlana is interested in creepy arts, observing dark sides of nature. Same time she is busy with making cartoonish illustrations for children, bright and full of love for life.
